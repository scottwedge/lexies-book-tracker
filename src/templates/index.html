Welcome to my book tracker!

This is a small app I created to track the books I’m reading. In this app, I can:

  • Write down what books I want to reading, and why I want to read them
  • Keep track of what I’m currently reading
  • Write mini-reviews of the books I've read

You can see what I’m reading, or create an account to track your own books.

If you’re looking for a way to track your own books, you may prefer something more like Goodreads, LibraryThing, or a spreadsheet you designed yourself.
